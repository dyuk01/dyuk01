## About Me

I am a Purdue Computer Science graduate based in Austin, Texas, specializing in software engineering. As Technical Lead at SingularX, I own the end-to-end architecture and development of a municipal permit platform built from scratch with React, Node.js, and Express.js.

My work spans production full-stack systems, data-intensive applications, and real-time computer vision. Previously, I developed a YOLOv11 and OpenCV tracking pipeline at CSI Vision and internationalization pipelines serving 100,000+ users at Naver Cloud.
